Hamilton Anxiety Rating Scale

Rate each item from 0 to 4. This HAM-A score tool is informational and intended to support, not replace, assessment by a qualified clinician.

01Anxious mood

Worries, anticipation of the worst, fearful anticipation, irritability.

02Tension

Feelings of tension, fatigability, startle response, trembling, restlessness, inability to relax.

03Fears

Fear of dark, strangers, being alone, animals, traffic, crowds, or other situations.

04Insomnia

Difficulty falling asleep, broken sleep, fatigue on waking, dreams, nightmares, night terrors.

05Intellectual

Difficulty concentrating or poor memory.

06Depressed mood

Loss of interest, lack of pleasure, depression, early waking, diurnal swing.

07Somatic muscular

Pains, aches, twitching, stiffness, jerks, grinding of teeth, unsteady voice, increased tone.

08Somatic sensory

Tinnitus, blurred vision, hot or cold flushes, weakness, prickling sensation.

09Cardiovascular symptoms

Rapid heartbeat, palpitations, chest pain, throbbing vessels, faintness, missed beats.

10Respiratory symptoms

Chest pressure or constriction, choking feelings, sighing, shortness of breath.

11Gastrointestinal symptoms

Difficulty swallowing, abdominal discomfort, nausea, vomiting, bowel changes, weight loss.

12Genitourinary symptoms

Urinary frequency or urgency, menstrual changes, sexual dysfunction, loss of libido.

13Autonomic symptoms

Dry mouth, flushing, pallor, sweating, dizziness, tension headache, hair raising.

14Behavior at interview

Fidgeting, restlessness, tremor, furrowed brow, strained face, sighing, rapid respiration.
